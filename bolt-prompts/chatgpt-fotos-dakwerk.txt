CHATGPT FOTOPROMPTS — DAKWERK LIMBURG
======================================
Gebruik deze prompts in ChatGPT (DALL-E) of Midjourney.
Tip: Midjourney v6 geeft betere realistische resultaten dan DALL-E.
Voeg altijd toe aan het einde: "photojournalism style, Canon DSLR, natural grain, no AI glow"

======================================
PROMPT 1 — HERO (homepage achtergrond)
======================================

Photorealistic photograph of a Belgian roofer working on a pitched roof.
The worker is kneeling on clay roof tiles, wearing an orange safety vest,
worn work gloves, and a safety harness attached to a rope. He is nailing
down a tile with a real nail gun. The tiles are slightly weathered, not
brand new. The roof has small imperfections: one slightly misaligned tile,
some moss in the corner. Overcast Belgian daylight, soft shadows, no
dramatic rays of sun. Background shows Flemish terraced houses and a
grey-white sky. Tools and material bags visible on the roof.
Shot with Canon 5D, 35mm lens, natural depth of field.
No AI glow, no synthetic textures, no perfect symmetry.

======================================
PROMPT 2 — DAKRENOVATIE (dienstkaart)
======================================

Photorealistic close-up of a roofer replacing old clay roof tiles on a
Belgian residential home. One worker is lifting an old cracked tile,
another is sliding a new terracotta tile into place. Wooden battens
visible underneath. Slightly dirty hands, real construction debris on
the roof. Natural overcast light, shallow depth of field focusing on
the hands and tiles. The tiles have varying shades of terracotta —
not uniform, not CGI-perfect. Real Flemish house architecture in the
soft background. Shot with a 50mm lens, natural grain, documentary style.

======================================
PROMPT 3 — PLAT DAK (dienstkaart)
======================================

Photorealistic photograph of a roofer applying EPDM rubber membrane
on a flat roof. The worker is using a heavy roller to press the seam flat.
The EPDM sheet is slightly wrinkled at the edge — not perfectly smooth.
A heat welding gun lies nearby. Safety boots, worn trousers with stains.
Overcast daylight. The flat roof shows a rooftop vent and a drain.
Visible brush marks from primer, realistic adhesive pooling at edges.
Belgian residential building visible in background.
No computer graphics, no perfect symmetry, natural photo grain.

======================================
PROMPT 4 — DAKISOLATIE (dienstkaart)
======================================

Photorealistic photograph showing roof insulation work from inside an attic.
A worker in dust mask and safety glasses is pressing rigid PIR insulation
boards between roof rafters. Old wooden beams, some dust particles in the
air caught by daylight coming through a small skylight. The insulation boards
are slightly cut and fitted — not CGI-perfect edges. Work lamp on the floor.
The attic shows signs of real use: old nails, dust, original structure.
Natural indoor lighting with a single work light, realistic grain.
No artificial glow, no studio lighting.

======================================
PROMPT 5 — GOTEN & ZINKWERK (dienstkaart)
======================================

Photorealistic photograph of a roofer installing a new zinc gutter on a
Belgian brick home. The worker is standing on a ladder, wearing work gloves,
using a rivet gun to fasten the gutter bracket to the fascia board.
The new grey zinc gutter contrasts with the slightly weathered brick wall.
Old gutter clip marks visible on the fascia. Autumn leaves on the roof edge.
Natural daylight, some cloud cover. The house has typical Flemish brick
architecture. Tools in a belt pouch. Real hands with visible work lines.
Shot with 35mm, natural depth of field, no studio lighting.

======================================
PROMPT 6 — STORMSCHADE (dienstkaart)
======================================

Photorealistic photograph of a damaged roof after a storm in Belgium.
Several clay roof tiles are dislodged or cracked, one section shows exposed
roof underlayment. A roofer in hi-vis vest is inspecting the damage while
another places a temporary weatherproof tarpaulin over the exposed area.
Wet tiles, overcast stormy sky, some broken branches nearby. Realistic damage:
not catastrophic destruction, just real storm impact on a Belgian residential
roof. Emergency feel, professional response. Documentary-style photography,
no dramatic movie lighting, no CGI.

======================================
EXTRA TIPS
======================================

Als het resultaat te clean/AI-achtig is:
→ Voeg toe: "add weathering, imperfections, real-world grime, uneven surfaces"
→ Voeg toe: "shot on location, not staged, candid work photo"

Zeg altijd:
→ "Make it look like a real photo, NOT AI-generated"
→ "photojournalism style, natural grain, no synthetic textures"

Vermijd in je prompt:
→ "perfect", "beautiful", "stunning", "dramatic lighting", "cinematic"
→ Die woorden sturen AI richting té clean output

Beste tools voor realistische resultaten:
→ Midjourney v6 (beste)
→ Adobe Firefly (goede optie)
→ DALL-E 3 (gaat, maar meer kans op AI-look)
